# HeadSoccerGame

A fun, fast-paced **HEAD SOCCER** game built with HTML5 Canvas. Use your head to score goals!

## How to Play

### Controls

**Player 1 (Blue)**
- **A/D** — Move left/right
- **W** — Jump
- **SPACE** — Power kick

**Player 2 (Red)**
- **←/→** — Move left/right
- **↑** — Jump
- **M** — Power kick

### Objective
Score **5 goals** to win! Head the ball into your opponent's goal. The ball can only enter the goal from below (through the bottom), not from the top.

### Game Mechanics
- **Jump** to reach higher balls and perform aerial headers
- **Power Kick** charges up and unleashes a powerful shot
- **Physics-based** ball movement with realistic bounces
- **Post deflection** — the ball bounces off goal posts and crossbars
- **Gravity** affects the ball realistically

### Goal Structure
- Goals are located on each side of the field
- The crossbar (top of goal) blocks balls coming from above
- Only balls entering from below count as goals
- Rebound off posts and crossbars for strategic plays

## Features
- Smooth 60fps canvas-based gameplay
- Beautiful night sky with animated stars
- Realistic physics engine
- Two-player local multiplayer
- Goal celebrations with audio feedback
- First to 5 wins!
